About

Full Biography

I'm a high school student with a wide range of passions that span creativity, technology, and athletics. Academically driven and naturally curious, I'm always pushing myself to explore new fields and sharpen new skills.

On the creative side, I'm a multi-instrumentalist — playing drums, piano, and guitar — and I also produce original music using BandLab. My love for content creation extends to YouTube, where I apply my video editing skills to bring ideas to life on screen. I'm currently expanding into 3D modeling and animation, adding yet another dimension to my creative toolkit.

Beyond the arts, I have a strong foothold in technology. I'm an AI enthusiast who keeps up with the latest developments in the field, and I can build and assemble PCs from the ground up. Whether it's hardware or software, I enjoy understanding how things work under the hood.

I'm also a writer and communicator — crafting essays, delivering speeches, and working as a translator across languages. In my downtime, I stay active through sports, balancing the mental demands of academics and creativity with physical discipline.

From music production to machine learning, video editing to essay writing — I bring dedication and versatility to everything I do.
